== The {sch} Equation

* {q-num} Show that the uncertainty principle does not apply to position and momentum components along different axes, e.g., that ++\(x\)++ and ++\(p_y\)++ are compatible operators and so ++\(\sigma_x\sigma_{p_y}\)++ can equal 0.
